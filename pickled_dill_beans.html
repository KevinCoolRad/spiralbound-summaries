Easy Pickled Dill Beans – Vintage Recipe for Crunchy Summer Snacking

They’re like a salad, a veggie, and a pickle all in one.

Vintage Pickled Dill Beans Recipe

2 1/2 c. vinegar
2 1/2 c. water
1/4 c. salt
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
2 cloves garlic
4 heads dill
2 1/2 lbs. green beans (tips off)

Boil vinegar, water, salt, and cayenne together. Put 1 garlic clove and 2 heads dill in each sterilized quart jar. Pack green beans upright into jars. Pour boiling liquid over beans, seal. Process 10 minutes in boiling water bath.
